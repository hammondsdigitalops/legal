Privacy Policy

Last updated July 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Hammonds Digital Ops ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit hammondsdigitalops.com, contact us, or opt in to receive text messages from us. By using our services or providing your information, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

01 Information we collect

We collect information you give us directly and a limited amount of technical information automatically:

  • Contact details you provide — your name, mobile phone number, and email address when you request information, book an appointment, or reply to our messages.
  • Communications — the content of text messages, calls, and emails between you and us.
  • Website usage data — basic technical information such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited, collected automatically when you use our website.

02 How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide our services;
  • Schedule, confirm, and follow up on appointments;
  • Send you text messages you have opted in to receive;
  • Operate, maintain, and improve our website and services;
  • Comply with our legal and regulatory obligations.

03 Text messaging & your consent

When you opt in to our text messaging program, we use your mobile phone number to send the messages you requested — such as appointment scheduling, confirmations, follow-ups, and offers. Your consent to receive text messages is not a condition of any purchase.

Your mobile data stays private

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. We do not sell, rent, or trade your mobile phone number or SMS opt-in data. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent are never shared with any third parties.

04 How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these limited situations:

  • Service providers who help us operate our business — for example, our text-messaging platform — and who are contractually bound to keep your information confidential and use it only to provide services to us;
  • Legal authorities, when we are required to do so by law or valid legal process.

As stated in Section 03, we never share mobile phone numbers or SMS consent with third parties or affiliates for their own marketing purposes.

05 Your choices & opting out

You are in control of the messages you receive from us:

  • Reply STOP to any text message to unsubscribe at any time.
  • Reply HELP, or email us at hammondsdigitalops@gmail.com, for assistance.
  • Contact us to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you.

06 Data security & retention

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect your information. We keep it only for as long as needed to provide our services, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

07 Children's privacy

Our services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18 years of age. If you believe a minor has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it.

08 Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of our services after any change means you accept the updated policy.
